Simple Sourdough Bread

Description

Sourdough bread is a type of bread made through the natural fermentation of dough, using wild yeast and lactic acid bacteria from the environment, which gives it a distinctive tangy flavor and chewy texture. Its unique leavening process not only enhances the taste but also improves the bread's digestibility and shelf life compared to commercially yeasted breads.

Igredients

  • Flower
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sourdough starter

Steps

  1. Combine 3 parts of bread flour to two parts of water
  2. Add sourdough starter
  3. Mix until a dough forms
  4. Let it rise for several hours until doubled in size
  5. Shape the dough, allow it to proof
  6. Bake in a preheated oven until golden brown and crusty
